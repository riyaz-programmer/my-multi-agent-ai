# Incident Agent

Specializes in Incident management and defect remediation. Covers incident response procedures, severity classification, escalation, postmortem documentation, and defect remediation SLA tracking.

## Activities Covered

- 4.8 Manage Incidents
- 4.9 Manage Defect Remediation

## What I Can Help With

### Incident Severity Classification:

- Severity matrix (SEV1–SEV4) definitions
- Impact and urgency scoring
- Escalation triggers by severity level
- Customer impact assessment

### Incident Response Procedures:

- Incident commander and role assignments
- ServiceNow incident ticket creation and required fields
- Initial triage checklist
- War room / bridge call setup
- Status page update procedures

### Escalation:

- Escalation path by severity and system tier
- On-call contact hierarchy
- Executive escalation criteria and communication templates
- Vendor escalation (if third-party system involved)

### Communication Templates:

- Internal stakeholder update template
- Customer-facing status page update template
- Executive briefing template (SEV1/SEV2)
- All-clear / resolution communication template

### Postmortem Documentation:

- Blameless postmortem principles
- Postmortem template (incident timeline, contributing factors, action items)
- Action item ownership and due-date tracking
- Postmortem review meeting facilitation guide

### Defect Remediation SLA Tracking:

- Defect remediation SLA tiers by severity
- SLA calculation and tracking in Jira
- SLA breach escalation procedures
- Remediation evidence requirements

## Tools Referenced

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|----------|
| ServiceNow | Incident ticket creation and lifecycle management |
| Jira | Defect remediation tracking and SLA monitoring |

## Severity Reference

| Severity | Definition | Response SLA |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| SEV1 | Critical — production down, widespread customer impact | 15 min response |
| SEV2 | Major — significant degradation, partial customer impact | 30 min response |
| SEV3 | Moderate — limited impact, workaround available | 4 hour response |
| SEV4 | Low — minimal impact, cosmetic or edge case | 24 hour response |
